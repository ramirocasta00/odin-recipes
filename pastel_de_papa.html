Pastel de Papa

Description

This dish consists of a layer of seasoned ground beef cooked with onions and spices, topped with mashed potatoes and baked until golden. Some variations include boiled eggs, cheese, or vegetables for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Mashed potatoes

Steps

  1. Season the ground beef with spices.
  2. Cook the ground beef with onions.
  3. Add mashed potatoes to the ground beef mixture.
  4. Bake the ground beef mixture until golden.
