Milanesa Recipe

Description

To make milanesas, thin slices of beef or chicken are seasoned, coated in flour, dipped in beaten eggs, and then covered with breadcrumbs. They are then fried in hot oil until golden and crispy. Some prefer baking them for a lighter version. Milanesas are often served with mashed potatoes, salad, or as a sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season the beef with salt and pepper.
  2. Coat the beef with flour.
  3. Beat the eggs.
  4. Coat the bread crumbs with flour.
